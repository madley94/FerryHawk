Aircraft Owners:

FerryHawk is a modern solution in finding qualified, experienced ferry pilot to move your aircraft. We vet our pilots to ensure credentials meet your expectations. Pilots:

FerryHawk’s platform enables you to view trips based upon your experience and location. Whether you’re looking at completing trips alongside an airline job, or you’re a full-time ferry pilot. Becoming a freelancer through FerryHawk allows the greatest flexibility with your time. Once accepting a trip with us, we will provide full operational support – improving efficiency for our clients while reducing pressure on you.
